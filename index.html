Classes

We offer a variety of classes to help you achieve your fitness goals.

Strength Training

Our strength training class focuses on building muscle and increasing overall strength. With a variety of weight lifting exercises and resistance training, this class will help you build lean muscle mass, increase bone density, and improve your overall strength and power.

  • Improved bone health
  • Increased muscle strength and tone
  • Prevents injury
  • Mental health benefits

Join our strength training class to improve your muscle strength for weigth lifting or powerlifting!

Cardio

Our cardio class is perfect for those who want to improve their cardiovascular health and endurance. With a variety of exercises such as running, cycling, and jumping rope, this class will help you improve your cardiovascular fitness and stamina.

  • Improved heart health
  • Increased endurance and stamina
  • Mood enhancement
  • Better sleep

Join our cardio class to make your heart healthy and improve your stamina!

Weight Loss

Our weight loss class is designed to help you shed unwanted pounds and achieve a healthier body weight. With a combination of cardiovascular exercises and strength training, this class will help you burn calories, build muscle, and boost your metabolism.

  • High-intensity interval training (HIIT)
  • Cardiovascular exercises like running, cycling, and rowing
  • Resistance training with weights and bodyweight exercises
  • Nutrition guidance and meal planning

Join our weight loss class to kickstart your weight loss journey and get the support you need to reach your goals!

Weight Gain

Our weight gain class is perfect for those looking to build muscle mass and increase their overall body weight. This class focuses on strength training and proper nutrition to help you pack on muscle and gain weight in a healthy and sustainable way.

  • Strength training with free weights and machines
  • Progressive overload techniques to increase muscle size and strength
  • Customized workout plans tailored to your goals and fitness level
  • Nutrition coaching and meal planning to support muscle growth

Join our weight gain class to transform your physique and achieve your desired weight and muscle mass!
